La historia de Kratos

God of War is an action-adventure game franchise created by David Jaffe at Sony's Santa Monica Studio. It began in 2005 on the PlayStation 2 (PS2) video game console, and has become a flagship title for the PlayStation brand, consisting of nine fully fledged games across multiple platforms. The series follows the Spartan warrior, Kratos, who is born a demigod and became the God of War.
